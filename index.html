Welcome to Vanity Rites, where artistry meets experience! For the past 15 years, we've been dedicated to creating high-quality, custom tattoos that reflect your unique style and story. We are constantly honing our craft, combining classic artistry with modern technique to create stunning, one of a kind tattoos. All of us here ensure every piece is crafted with precision, passion, and professionalism. Whether you're looking for bold traditional work, intricate black/grey, vibrant colors, epic pieces or small, fine line or text, we're here to bring your vision to life. Marlena

Grounded in neo- and traditional styles, she takes ornamental and fine line to a whole new lever - hers. While she favors black work, botanical, symmetrical and vintage arrangements, her colors are flawless - there's nothing she can't do.

Ted

Classically trained and a graduate of Paier College of Art and the Art Student's League in NY. After studying art in Italy, he came back and put all that knowledge and skills to work tattooing. Ted harbors professionalism, attention to detail, and composition knowledge.

Rae

Another Paier College of Art alum. Rae has spent their appreticeship proving that “good enough” really isn't in his vocabulary. With a sharp eye for detail and a passion for the craft, Rae has officially moved into the chair and is ready to take on clients. Whether it's the abstract precision of woodblock style, the epic scale of fantasty art or his favorite style of YOUR favoirte anime icon(s), Rae breathes life into every piece. Specializing in high-contrast black and grey, he thrives on creative challenges. Your wild idea and his wild imagination makes for some awesome tattoos!

Andrew

As a medical liason, Andrew helps tattoo artists elevate their standards of care and client safety. As an educator, he consults not only with studios, but clients alike on best practices and safety protocols - as it pertains to tattoo and skin health and facilitates certification training. Andrew provides the safest, most professional experience.

Laura

Laura, an emerging talent in our program, a creator who understands the human form deeply. Whether hiking or beading intricate patterns, Laura knows exactly how the body handles tattoos, ensuring your experience is as comfortable as it is transformative, seated at the intersection of organic and eerie.
